# Activities

Five unplugged paper activities that pair with the workbook. Designed to work without WiFi, computers, or a teacher.

## Inventory

| ID | Activity | Spread | Time | Skill |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| A1 | [Sort by feature](A1-sort-by-feature.md) | 5 | 10 min | Pattern recognition; what features are |
| A2 | [Build a tiny dataset](A2-build-a-tiny-dataset.md) | 8 | 15 min | Where data comes from; what's missing |
| A3 | [Prompt redesign](A3-prompt-redesign.md) | 10 | 15 min | Asking AI clearly; iterating |
| A4 | [Spot the AI](A4-spot-the-ai.md) | 13 | 20 min | Recognizing AI-generated content |
| A5 | [Family conversation guide](A5-family-conversation-guide.md) | 15 | open | Talking with family about AI |

## Design principles

Every activity must:
- Be completable with a pencil and the packet itself
- Take 10–20 minutes (A5 is open-ended)
- Produce a tangible output the youth can keep
- Work in either English or Spanish without translation overhead
- Lead with a question, not a definition
- Include a reflection prompt that reaches past the literal task

## Bilingual

Activities are content-light and visual-heavy by design, so Spanish translation is structural, not voluminous. Translation register is validated by a paid community translator at v0.9.
