# Partner reprint kit

If you are a library, school, district, family resource center, parish youth program, or sibling nonprofit and you want to reprint and distribute these packets in your community — **you don't need permission**. The CC BY-SA 4.0 license is a permanent invitation. This directory exists to make reprinting as easy as possible.

## Status

Pre-v1.0 — full reprint kit ships with v1.0 release (~September 2026).

## What will be in this kit at v1.0

- Print-ready PDFs (full bleed, professional press / risograph / docucenter)
- Cover-only PDF for low-volume on-demand printing
- Co-branding template — drop your logo on the back-cover slot
- Bilingual partner one-pager describing the project to your board/community
- Suggested distribution placements (where to put packets in your space so youth find them)
- Recipient-feedback postcard template (return-to-DROPOUT-Labs or return-to-you)

## Already want to reprint?

Until v1.0 ships, you can still:
- Open a [Reprint Request issue](https://github.com/dropout-labs/ai-literacy-packets/issues/new?template=reprint-request.yml)
- Email morgan@dropoutlabs.dev
- Sign up for a notification when v1.0 lands at [dropoutlabs.dev](https://dropoutlabs.dev)

## License terms (in plain language)

You can reprint, remix, translate, and distribute. You do not owe DROPOUT Labs money. You should:
- Keep credit somewhere (back cover, colophon, "originally created by DROPOUT Labs")
- License your version under the same CC BY-SA 4.0
- Tell us, if you want, so we can celebrate it

That's it.
