# Spread 15 — Family conversation guide

> Back matter · spread 15 of 16 · Activity A5

## Heading

**Talk with your family.**

## Body

This page is for talking, not writing.

Find someone in your family — anyone — and read these out loud together. You don't have to do all of them. Pick the ones you want to talk about.

If you write anything, keep it short. The point is the talking.

> *If there is no one at home you want to do this with, that is okay. Talk with a friend, a teacher, a librarian, or no one — just think about the questions.*

## Margin note (small)

> The order of these prompts isn't random.
>
> The first ones invite the older person to share *their* expertise about scams and manipulation. They have plenty.
>
> The middle ones connect their existing instincts to AI-specific things.
>
> The last ones flip it — the youth can teach back.
>
> The arc is on purpose. Try them in order if you can.
