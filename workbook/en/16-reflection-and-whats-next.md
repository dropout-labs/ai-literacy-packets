## What's next

There are AI tools you can try right now. Some are free. Some have free tiers. Use them with what you learned in this packet — especially the asking page (spread 9) and the trust page (spread 11).

Tools worth trying *(safe to use as of 2026; check before you go in)*:

- **A general-purpose AI** like ChatGPT, Claude, or Gemini — for asking, writing, brainstorming.
- **A search-AI** like Perplexity — for questions where you want sources you can check.
- **An image AI** like one of the free image generators — for art and exploration.

> **Before you use any of them, read the privacy notice.** Your conversations may be used to train future AI. Don't share information about other people. Don't share things you'd be sad to see leak.
