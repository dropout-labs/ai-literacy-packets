## Editorial conventions

- 6th-grade reading level (Flesch-Kincaid grade ≤ 6)
- Asks more than tells (each spread leads with a question or scenario)
- No vocabulary used without plain-language explanation
- Honest about AI: not hyped, not catastrophized
- Family-inclusive (a parent or older sibling can engage alongside)
